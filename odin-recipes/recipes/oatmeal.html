Oatmeal

Description

Simple oatmeal recipe.
Preparation time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Oats of your choice
  • Water or milk

Preparation

  1. Pour oats into the pan
  2. Add 300ml of water or milk
  3. Bring to a boil
  4. Turn down the heat
  5. Stir well until the oats get to your desired consistency
