Chicken breast

Description

Simple, clean and low calorie chicken breast recipe.
Preparation time: ~30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Spices
  • Oil

Preparation

  1. Wash and cut the chicken breast into medium-sized stripes
  2. Drop two tablespoons of oil on to the pan, turn on the heat
  3. Once the pan has heated up, lay the cut up breasts on the pan
  4. Add desired spices, for example salt, herbal pepper and cayenne pepper
  5. Cook the breasts for twenty minutes with the lid on, flip every 3-5 minutes
